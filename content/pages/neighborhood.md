## Your New Backyard (Well, Close Enough)

Grace Street Towers sits in the heart of East Lakeview — one of Chicago's most walkable and vibrant lakefront neighborhoods. Bordered by Lake Michigan, Lincoln Park, and the energy of Wrigleyville and Boystown, you'll never run out of things to do.

The Lakefront Trail is steps away for morning runs and weekend bike rides. Belmont Harbor offers paddleboarding, a dog beach, and the Bill Jarvis Migratory Bird Sanctuary. Shop the boutiques of Broadway and Northalsted, catch a Cubs game at Wrigley Field, or explore over 30 local theaters and live music venues like Kingston Mines and Metro.
